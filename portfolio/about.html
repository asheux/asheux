Interests

I am fascinated by the human brain and how it works. My fascination with the brain grew as a result of studying computation in machine. My interest is to understand how the brain works computationally, and my main area of study is attention and memory, specifically visual attention in both humans and animals. This is in hopes that studying attention will give us clues to how, where and why consciousness emerges in the brain.

My research questions include:

  • How is visual attention related to memory retention?
  • How does attention affect working memory in humans?
  • Is there any correlation between visual attention and attention required to complete a cognitive task?
  • What is the neural correlates of attention?
  • And, how does attention relate to dreams?
