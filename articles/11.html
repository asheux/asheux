Love and The Universe

The nature of the universe and the doubts in my mind
Are fascinating,
I think of everything in the former to be the
Cause of understanding the latter,
My perception of the former has in doubt led me
To the latter and thereafter, provoked my way of thinking.

I know not what to make of real,
Reality has, to me, become a realm of questions,
What is real?
What is love in the realm of the universe?
Is there more to the physical world, the tangible?
These inquiries and more led me to reality.

The philosophy of love,
The question of, what is it, has in fact led me
To the answers I seek,
A soul in the universe,
Within the body of the physical world.

A soul that makes the world within me real,
If happiness is real,
Doubts real,
Universe real,
Love real,
I real,
Then, she is real.

The true definition of love exists within her,
An idea which to me has been a mystery,
A mystery that a single soul with perfection,
and imperfection within, helped solve,
To both, I can now with no doubts say I understand.
