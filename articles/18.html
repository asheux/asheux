What am I?

The void I so want to fill
With nothing or something
I do not understand.
It could be that the void exits
Because I myself make it so.
Perhaps something else takes away
The things that make I whole.
So that I shall not see what
Is hidden from I.

In the absence of such a thing
That takes from I,
Then it must be that I alone
unconsciously gives away the
Things dear to I.
The very things that fill the void.
Whether these things are replaceable,
I do not know.

I wonder,
What then shall I consume in order
To make it so that I shall not unconsciously
Lose that which makes I whole?
It is clear to I that such a thing exists,
Yet existence of it is far from the
Perception of I.

But to find that which the empty vessel
Seeks, I shall have to open the very
Eyes that sees the hidden
And forever shall I never lack consciousness.
