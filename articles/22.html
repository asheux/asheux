The Hunt

Today, I open a door.
Tomorrow, the hunt begins.

I become the hunted
and what came through becomes the hunter.

I wonder how much influence
the man upstairs has in this.
Does he allow the hunt to happen?
Maybe it’s his entertainment.

As long as I am under his roof
perhaps he permits the chase
because the chase is how I pay rent.

I want to stop the hunt
so badly I don’t mind the price.
Yet it’s almost impossible.

I close one door to keep the hunter out;
another opens
almost always.

Sometimes I wonder whether I’m his prisoner
but what kind of prison feels this free?

Maybe I’m alone, and the man upstairs
left this house for another.

I do not know.
