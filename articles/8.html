F**k red & blue. I want the green pill

200 and seventy days plugged in,
Maybe more, maybe less,
But still, very much plugged,
I remember not the song she sang me,
Not a word she spoke,
Sadly, not even the pain I caused her,
It’s unclear and perhaps logically
Implausible to have a total recall
Of those experiences,
Of what it feels to be her,
To be with her.

Should I or not think about it?
The moments we were one, a totality?
Some agents. Ooh! Those invisible agents,
Dare offer me the red or blue pill,
Please! I know where those leads,
Zion or bliss; nowhere,
Nowhere close to those sweet little
Me and her moments,
Science gives me the concept of it,
The gods haven’t tried,
It’s just me and my memory now.

Take me back,
Back to the moment when it all began,
Let me feel your pain,
Hear the songs,
Comprehend your struggle,
Let me be you,
Smell you, kick you,
Let me love you like you did
The unconscious poor little me,
Let me have the green pill.
