The Universe Within

ou and I are merely ideas in the universe,
First contact, I seem to know little
About you, and you as I,
But what then is the I
That which perceives the you,
if not the I that knows you.

I perceive you as you do I,
To think that little is known about you to I,
is but an illogical conclusion,
The perception of you is merely what’s within I,
As you perceive I what’s within in itself.

It is beauty,
It is happiness,
It is intelligence,
It is peace,
And it is all but evil,
That which I perceive within you.

The idea of you and I,
That which the universe intelligently designed,
now makes total sense to me,
Because I cannot exist without
the subjective experience of you.
