The Prison

Certainly, my conscious experiences
have no limit. So I thought,
Could be the sky,
Could be the other side,
Perhaps, It could be my own undoing,
But no, it is the observer,
A bold claim you would say, but what if?

The space is vast,
My curiosity like that of a child,
But only familiarity I see amid my
explorations,
Strangely, the followers are the only
Strangers with the same uncertainty as mine,
My perception is far greater than my being
Yet what I can touch is only limited
By the walls. Which with motives,
Blocks me from the other side.

It’s pretty out there,
It could be deceit by my own eyes,
Which contradicts with my own
Beliefs about reality,
Is what’s within the walls
the only reality in my reach?
Is what’s beyond not real at all?
It’s quite illogical to infer
that which is out of my reach as illusory,
Because they are real,
Real as the processes in my mind.

Am I a prisoner in my own mind?
Or is my mind a prisoner to something else;
The observer?
