Unexplored light

Lately, I've been exploring otherworlds,
Mostly the darkside with an absolute open mind,
Objectively, never to follow ideas in them to their birth.
Intrigued, I was,
By the nature of the darkside,
Which is contrary to the good old stories(GOS).
Determinism had me believe that in
The dark we find merely darkness,
Yet my encounters have been pure light.
Unexplored light which has nothing to
Shine on except the darkness.
The light is searching,
Looking for what most do; answers.
Questions, it has lots,
Doubts plenty,
But mostly, questions with potential uncertainties.
Some worlds are unknowingly naive,
but most are self-aware like many
who see the light In its true nature.
Imprisoned by ignorance, they are.
If I could break them out, I would,
But what use am I, when I am a prisoner myself?
Luckily, I got one question answered,
There is less uncertainties in the dark
And more in the light.
